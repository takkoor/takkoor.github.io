Policy Insight Tool

Designing a policy decision-support experience

University administrators often make high-stakes decisions using fragmented and inconsistent policy information. This project reframed a large-scale policy analysis as a product design problem: how might we reduce cognitive load while enabling meaningful comparison?

My role: Research, product design, information architecture, data visualization

Outcome: Policy brief, stakeholder presentation, conceptual dashboard
